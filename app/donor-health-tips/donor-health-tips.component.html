Before Donation

  • Get adequate sleep (7-8 hours)
  • Eat a healthy meal within 3 hours of donation
  • Drink plenty of water
  • Avoid fatty foods before donation

Nutrition Guidelines

Iron-Rich Foods

Include lean meats, spinach, and legumes

Vitamins

Consume citrus fruits and vegetables

Hydration

Drink 8-10 glasses of water daily

Post-Donation Care

Rest Well

Take it easy for the next 24 hours

Stay Hydrated

Increase fluid intake for 48 hours

Avoid Heavy Exercise

Skip strenuous activities for 24 hours
